Jollof rice

Recipy for jollof

Jollof is a west African dish of rice cooked in a sauce of tomatoes and onions seasoned with various spices and often accompanied by meat, fish or vegetables.

Ingredients for Jollof

  • Rice
  • Pepper mix
  • Tomato paste
  • Cooking oil
  • Onions
  • Seasoning powders
  • Salt
  • Carrots
  • Meat

Steps for making jollof rice

  1. Wash the meat properly and rinse it repeatedly in a clean water
  2. Chop it into chewable pieces and add into a medium or large pot
  3. Add 1 large onion and some salt then cover with water to heat for sometime
  4. Let it boil for anything between 45-50 minutes while on medium heat
  5. Turn off the stove and set aside the meat broth to rest
  6. On the same pot, add onions and fry them untill they become soft and brownnish
  7. add a few garlic cloves and cook all together with the onions for some minutes while stirring continuously
  8. Add the meat and let it simmer on low heat for about an hour
  9. Blend tomatoes and the rest of the garlic untill they form a thuck paste. Transfer them to a separate bowl
  10. In a pan, saute some oil over medium or low heat
  11. Add the onion paste and cook untill the water evaporates and they start turning brown
  12. Pour in the tomato puree then add in some salt and add other spices
  13. Add the meat and broth then cook for 15-20 minutes while occasionally stirring untill the stew turns deep red
  14. Add in the rice, vegetables and just enough water to over the mixture
  15. Bring the mix to boil the cook on a low or medium heat for 25-30 minutes

Once all the water has evaporated from the rice, you can now serve it with your favourite soft drink or juice.
